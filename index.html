My Abyss

My Abyss is a "mystery themed turn-based RPG" inspired by the likes of Yume Nikki, Omori, Undertale, and Ace Attorney. In this game you play as a lone girl who lost all of her memories as she explores the estranged archepelago she has found herself in.

Throughout the game, you'll encounter many new faces, explore esoteric dungeons, and uncover the truth behind the mysteries buried underneath the surface.
